Tools are big business

People buy tools to fix things. There are many types of tools to buy. Tools are used in a various industries and contribute to reducing the amount of time completing tasks. In addition, there are different levels of quality for tools. Hardware stores have similiar approaches to selling tools.

Two of the biggest competitors in the market with Home Depot and Lowes, sell similiar products but under different brands. Quality could differ as well but generally the tools sold at both stores are comparable.
